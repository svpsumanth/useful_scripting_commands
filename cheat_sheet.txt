1) q!		-	Close without saving
2) x!		-	Saving and closing the file
3) set number	-	Displays line numbers on the left 
4) <LineNumber>	-	Jumps to that line 
5) $ 		-	Jumps to last line 
6) dd(no ":")	- 	Deletes that line and move everything by a single line
7) A		- 	Insert text at the end of the line
8) o		-	Inserts text in next line
